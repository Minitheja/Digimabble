How does a project with Digi Mabble work?

+
A project with Digi Mabble typically follows a structured corporate workflow designed to ensure smooth communication, timely delivery, and high-quality results. We coordinate with your technical stakeholders to map processes, establish secure API interfaces, build prototype agents, and deploy with complete oversight.

How long does an AI or automation project usually take?

+
The duration of an AI or automation project depends on the complexity, data availability, and scope of automation. Basic workflow automations and chat/voice integrations can be operational in 2 to 4 weeks, whereas custom enterprise model fine-tuning and proprietary pipeline integrations may take 6 to 12 weeks.

Do you work with small, medium, and large companies?

+
Yes. We work with businesses of all sizes—growing startups, mid-sized companies, and large corporate enterprises. Our approach is flexible and scalable, allowing us to tailor our solutions based on the client’s goals, budget, compliance structures, and technical requirements.

Are your solutions GDPR compliant?

+
Yes, all our solutions are designed to be fully GDPR compliant. We host pipelines on secure cloud configurations, implement data encryption in transit and at rest, and establish filters that prevent personal identifiable information (PII) from being stored or exposed to external AI model logs.

Can I discuss my idea directly with a team member?

+
Yes, absolutely. You can discuss your business needs directly with an AI Solutions Engineer. We schedule a discovery call to review your current tool stack, analyze automation opportunities, and discuss feasibility, timeline, and security compliance.
